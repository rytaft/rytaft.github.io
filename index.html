I am a second-year PhD student in the Databases Group at MIT, working with Mike Stonebraker and Sam Madden.  I graduated from Yale University in 2008 with a B.S. in Physics.  After that, I spent two years working as a consultant at Exeter Group, and another two years working as a software engineer at Bloomberg LP.  My current research interests include the application of databases to scientific workloads, as well as elastic scalability of DBMSs.
