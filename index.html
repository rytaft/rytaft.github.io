Rebecca Taft rytaft@alum.mit.edu

I graduated from MIT with a PhD in Computer Science in September, 2017, and now I am a Software Engineer at Cockroach Labs. While at MIT, I worked in the Database Group with Mike Stonebraker. My thesis research focused on elastic scalability of DBMSs as well as multi-tenancy in the cloud.
